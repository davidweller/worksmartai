<p>Dave&nbsp;and&nbsp;I&nbsp;have had&nbsp;a&nbsp;fantastic afternoon at the Tech South West’s&nbsp;AI&nbsp;South West&nbsp;Live event in Taunton.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

<p>The&nbsp;sold-out&nbsp;event was attended by around a hundred of the region’s AI founders, innovators,&nbsp;educators&nbsp;and technologists-&nbsp;meaning that&nbsp;conversations&nbsp;were incredibly interesting and presentations varied and insightful.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

<p>It was great to see the excitement and buzz from attendees towards the possibilities of AI in&nbsp;the&nbsp;future, but with the huge&nbsp;caveat&nbsp;that many are also&nbsp;slightly terrified –&nbsp;proceed&nbsp;with caution and understanding was the clear takeaway of the event!&nbsp;</p>

<p>Our presentation on how to&nbsp;practically approach&nbsp;adopting AI into&nbsp;the&nbsp;workforce was met with a lot&nbsp;of interest, with many follow-up questions and discussions&nbsp;afterwards about&nbsp;our core message&nbsp;&#8211;&nbsp;the importance of remembering “the people”.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

<p>As we see so often&nbsp;when working with clients, the&nbsp;initial&nbsp;thoughts and considerations are given to the technology, and not to the staff who are going to be using it!&nbsp;</p>

<p>Already looking forward to the next event in September.&nbsp;  </p>
