Frequently Asked Questions

What makes WorkSmart-AI different from other AI training providers?

We focus on people-first adoption – helping staff feel confident and productive with AI and supporting leadership teams to make strategic data-driven implementation decisions. 

As AI specialists within higher education, we understand the needs and challenges experienced by staff, and are here to support you in overcoming them. Rather than just demonstrating tools, our training connects AI use to real measurable outcomes, like time saved, improved productivity, and better policy alignment.

We also personalise our training to adhere to individual institutions AI and data policies.

Who are your services designed for?

Our core audience is universities and education providers – supporting both academic and professional services staff. We also work with local councils, public sector bodies, and SMEs across the South West who want to build practical AI capability and confidence.

How do you personalise training for each organisation?

Every project begins with a free consultation. We then continue with a needs analysis to identify your current use of AI, challenges, and goals. From there, we design a tailored roadmap that ensures all workshops, online courses, and resources address your specific context, policies and priorities. Through regular data-monitoring, we’re able to iterate training to ensure that outcomes are fully achieved.

What topics do you cover in training?

Our training covers a range of practical and strategic areas, including:

  • AI for research and academic workflows
  • AI fundamentals and safe, ethical use
  • Productivity and process improvement
  • Teaching and assessment with AI
  • Policy development and governance
  • Environmental impact and sustainability considerations
Can you deliver training in person as well as online?

Yes. We offer a mix of in-person workshops, live online webinars, and asynchronous on-demand courses. Most clients choose a blended approach so staff can learn flexibly and apply their learning right away.

How do you measure the impact of your training?

We track both staff engagement and tangible outcomes, such as hours saved, efficiency gains, and staff confidence. For larger partnerships, we provide dashboards and reports showing the value of AI adoption over time.

What’s included in your programmes?

All packages include:

  • Free initial consultation
  • Staff training needs analysis
  • Personalised roadmap and action plan
  • Access to online learning resources
  • Expert-led workshops
  • Pedagogical best practice guidance
  • Ongoing support
  • Monthly online drop-in sessions
  • Follow-up review and next steps

If you’re interested in specific tools or training that you don’t see here, please let us know – we’ll be able to work with you to ensure your priorities are covered in our training.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. Beyond one-off training, we provide Training-as-a-Service retainers for continuous AI upskilling, policy development, and impact measurement – ensuring progress and implementation doesn’t stop after the workshops.

How do we get started?

Book a free consultation to discuss your goals. We’ll help you assess your organisation’s AI readiness, identify the best training approach, and design a package that delivers measurable results.
