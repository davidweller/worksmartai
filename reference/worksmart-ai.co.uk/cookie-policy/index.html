Cookie Policy

Effective date: 20th October 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how WorkSmart-AI (“we”, “our”, “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. They allow websites to function properly, improve performance, and provide information to site owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following categories of cookies:

a. Essential Cookies

  • Necessary for the website to function (e.g. security, page navigation, form submission).
  • Without these, the site cannot operate correctly.
  • These do not require your consent.

b. Analytics & Performance Cookies

  • Help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g. Google Analytics).
  • Provide insights on which pages are most popular, how long users stay, and where traffic comes from.
  • Data is aggregated and anonymous.

c. Functionality Cookies

  • Remember choices you make (e.g. language preference, form entries).
  • Improve user experience.

3. Third Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services we use, such as:

  • Google Analytics (website analytics).
  • Microsoft Forms (analytics)
  • Social Media Platforms (e.g. LinkedIn).

These providers may use cookies independently. We encourage you to review their privacy and cookie policies.

4. How to Manage Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject non-essential cookies.

  • On your first visit, our cookie banner will ask for your preferences.
  • You can update or withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings or re-opening the cookie banner.

Browser Controls:
Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually:

  • Delete existing cookies.
  • Block all cookies.
  • Allow only certain types.

Please note: Blocking essential cookies may affect the website’s functionality.

5. Duration of Cookies

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them.

We review our cookies regularly to ensure they remain necessary and proportionate.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website, with the “effective date” shown at the top.
